Princess Mononoke

Princess Mononoke is about a young prince who gets cursed by a corrupted pig god, and then is kicked out of his own village due to the curse.
He then goes on a journey to find a cure. The young prince will find many new friends on the way.~

Howl's Moving Castle

This movie as about a young boring woman living her normal boring life, until howl comes and stirs it up. This however causes an evil old
whitch to cast an interesting curse on her because of howl. She then finds herself going on an adventure in search for this howl to get the curse removed.~

Spiritied Away

A young child and her parents end up lost on a road trip, somehow entering the spirit realm. Seeing as they do not belong the parents had
no idea that eating food in that world would cause them to turn into pigs!!!. It's up to the girl to save her parents!
